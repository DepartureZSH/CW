Course Enrollment Guide

Direct Method

  1. Find the course you are interested in.

  2. Click the 'Enroll' button to register for the course.

Indirect Method

  1. Find the course you are interested in.

  2. Click the 'Star' button to bookmark the course.

  3. Go to the 'My Modules' page and click the 'Enroll' button next to the starred course to register.

Alternative Method

  1. Find the instructor of the course you are interested in.

  2. Contact the instructor and request to be manually added to the course.
